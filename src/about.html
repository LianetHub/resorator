About us

How it all started

SHOCK WAVE is an ideal place for those who want to learn wakesurfing and get unforgettable impressions from active pastime on the water. Professional instructors will help you learn the basics of wakesurfing and achieve the desired level of skill.

It helps you to develop not only physical fitness and balance, but also to enjoy the water and nature. Immersing yourself in the atmosphere of excitement and freedom, you will be able to relax, take a break from everyday hustle and bustle and find a new hobby that will fill your life balance.

Professional instructors will help you develop technique and confidence on the water, and the unique atmosphere will create an unforgettable experience.

equipment

  • Wakesurfing equipment is designed to keep the rider safe and comfortable. Basic elements include:
  • Life jacket: Keeps the rider afloat in the event of a fall. Fits well and provides adequate buoyancy.
  • Helmet: Protects the rider's head from hitting the water or board. Meets safety standards and fits comfortably.
  • Gloves: Protect the rider's hands from blisters and cuts. With good grip and sun protection.
  • Wetsuit: Protects the rider from ultraviolet radiation.
  • Using proper wakesurfing equipment greatly reduces the risk of injury and ensures a safe and enjoyable surfing experience.

Our spot

On the shores of the Mediterranean Sea, in sunny Limassol, Cyprus is our Wakesurfing School. Here you can immerse yourself in the world of exciting water sports and experience the unforgettable sensation of gliding over the waves. Our experienced instructors will make the learning process easy and fun, and the picturesque views of the Cypriot coastline will be a great addition to your vacation. Join us and discover the world of wakesurfing by the sea!
